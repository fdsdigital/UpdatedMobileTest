Hoku S.

C&C is a cute Lil store, the restaurant located on Normandy in the Westside Plaza. They cater to American and Filipino dishes. On Saturday evenings they have Karaoke/dancing. It's a cute Lil place located in the corner right next to where Copacabana. If you are looking to book a small party you can call to make a reservation. From time to time they will have an AYCE buffet which is 10. per person. If you don't want the buffet they do have a menu you can order from. It is made fresh to order so it will be nice and hot. Karaoke would start from 8 pm till closing. Very cozy atmosphere, lots of parking.
